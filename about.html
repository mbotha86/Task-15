About Us

You and Me Design launched in 2016 with a focus on well custom built wood and steel furniture. Since 2020 we have expanded our range to include baby goods, hand crafted wooden goods and bespoke leather goods.

Our baby range includes dummy clips, teethers, bibs and sesnosry blankets. These can be purchased individually or in custom gift boxes.

Our leather range includes men's wallets and toiletry bags. Each one is hand cut and sewn for a quality finish.

All of our wooden products are handmade and one of a kind. While we do keep a few boards in stock, our wooden products are all made to order.

For examples of all our products, hop on over to our Instagram page, @you_and_me_design.
